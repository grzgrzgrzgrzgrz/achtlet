# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this project should stay direct, respectful, and useful.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a productive environment:

- giving actionable technical feedback
- assuming good intent while challenging ideas on their merits
- keeping criticism focused on code, behavior, or decisions
- helping newcomers understand repo conventions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- discriminatory language or exclusionary conduct
- derailing discussions with hostility or repeated bad-faith behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that
violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations can lead to temporary or
permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
privately. Use the same private contact path described in `SECURITY.md` when
needed.
